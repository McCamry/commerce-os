# 1. Record Architecture Decisions

Date: 2026-07-08

## Status
Accepted

## Context
We need to record the architectural decisions made on this project. As the project grows, team members will come and go. It's important to have a historical record of why certain technical decisions were made, what options were considered, and what consequences the decision has.

## Decision
We will use Architecture Decision Records (ADR). We will store them in the `docs/adr` directory as markdown files.
Each record will follow a standard format with Status, Context, Decision, and Consequences.

## Consequences
- Better onboarding for new developers.
- Less time spent re-arguing past decisions.
- A clear history of how the system architecture evolved.
